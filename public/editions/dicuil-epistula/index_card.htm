Dicuil, Epistula censuum

ed. Christian G. Schweizer (2025)

The Epistula censuum (‘Letter of dues’) was written by the Irish scholar Dicuil as a ‘due’ for the Carolingian emperor Louis the Pious around March 818 CE. It is a poem on weights and measures, combined with a prosaic treatise on homonyms and synonyms.
