v <De omonymis solummodo in diuersis causis inuentis>

1 |folio 14v l. 8 (P) Stagnum appellando aquam metallumque liquidum in singulari numero omonymum.

2 Sed hoc in plurali deficit omonymum fieri, sicut fori tantummodo nauium in singulari et forus contionandi in plurali.

3 Hoc propter defiecienten numerum iam pluralem, illa ne ob neutrum genus ac terminationem in uno singularem, in altero pluralem.[69]

Notes

  1. ^ In the manuscript, in altero pluralem can be found a second time after this, in faint script.
